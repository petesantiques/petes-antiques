Need Help with an Estate?

Have a house full of treasures or a large collection you need handled with care? Pete’s Antiques specializes in professionally managing estate auctions, from rare collectibles to full household contents. We operate in the Fort Collins/Denver area with attention to detail and respect for your family’s legacy.

We love telling the unique stories behind amazing items — it helps keep history alive and helps Pete’s Antiques get discovered by others who appreciate it! With your permission, we’d love to capture and share your story and treasures on platforms like YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram. Think of us like respectful reporters — showcasing the excitement of your finds without revealing sensitive information. We protect your privacy: full names, specific locations, and any personal details are always kept confidential unless you choose otherwise. Let’s show the world your cool stuff, safely and professionally!
