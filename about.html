Our Story

Pete’s Antiques has been a treasure trove of rare finds, collector’s dreams, and vintage charm since 1975. What started as one man’s passion for history became Fort Collins’ favorite stop for antiques, stories, and unique Americana.

Our Mission

Food. Water. Shelter. Stories. We believe that the relics of the past shape the stories of today and tomorrow. Every item that passes through Pete’s hands holds a piece of history — and deserves a new life.
